Sub Form View/Add/Edit

Clicking on the many-to-one relationship with the primary table. By clicking the edit or view button you opened the record as a sub form.

 

Add/Edit Record

A sub form has the same controls and functionality as a form and is edited in the same way.

 

View Record

The record can be viewed but not edited. Users with editing priviledges can edit the record by clicking the (form) button in the colored results bar to open the record in an editable form.
