Simple Search Results

Results from the chosen table are designated with a blue () bar

Results from related tables are designated with an aqua () bar

Results can be viewed by clicing the (Expand) icon.

Results are displayed as a Grid.

Manipulate the results by clicking an icon on the bar:

  •  Create a Record Set
  •  Open records; to open a specific record first choose the record then click on the icon, to open all the records select the icon without choosing a specific record
  •  Export the grid to MS Excel
  •  Print the results Grid
